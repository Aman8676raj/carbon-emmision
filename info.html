Recent News & Updates

April 15, 2025 • Environmental Policy

India Announces New Carbon Reduction Targets for 2030

The Indian government has unveiled ambitious new carbon reduction targets aiming to cut emissions by 45% from 2005 levels by 2030, strengthening its commitment to the Paris Agreement.

Source: Ministry of Environment
April 12, 2025 • Renewable Energy

Delhi Launches Massive Rooftop Solar Initiative

The Delhi government has launched a citywide rooftop solar program aiming to install 500 MW of solar capacity by 2027, offering substantial subsidies to residential and commercial buildings.

Source: Delhi Energy Department
April 10, 2025 • Transportation

Mumbai Expands Electric Bus Fleet to Reduce Emissions

Mumbai's transportation authority has added 300 new electric buses to its fleet, bringing the total to over 1,000 electric vehicles in public transport, expected to reduce carbon emissions by 30,000 tons annually.

Source: Mumbai Transport Corporation
April 8, 2025 • Research

New Study Links Air Pollution to Declining Crop Yields in Northern India

Researchers from the Indian Agricultural Research Institute have published findings showing that increasing air pollution in the Indo-Gangetic plain has reduced wheat and rice yields by up to 15% over the past decade.

Source: Environmental Science Journal
